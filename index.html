Frequently Asked Questions

Frequently asked questions about website creation, performance optimization, and the customer journey. Didn't find the answer you were looking for? Contact me for clarification.

1. How long does it take to create a website?

The time frame depends on the complexity of the project. Landing pages usually take 1-2 weeks, business card sites - about a month, and the development of an online store or a complex corporate site can take 2-3 months.

2. How much does it cost to develop a website?

The cost depends on the type of the site, its functionality and design. Prices start from 10,000 rubles for simple projects and can reach up to 100,000 rubles or more for large portals. An exact calculation is possible after discussing your requirements.

3. What technologies do you use in development?

I use modern tools and technologies such as HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript (including React or Vue frameworks), and Python/Django for the server side. The choice of technologies depends on the project objectives.

4. Will the site be adapted for mobile devices?

Yes, all websites are created taking into account the principles of responsive design so that they are displayed correctly on smartphones, tablets and computers.

5. Can I make changes to the site myself after it is developed?

Sure. I can integrate a content management system (CMS) like WordPress or a custom admin panel. This will allow you to add new pages, edit text or images without having to ask for help.
